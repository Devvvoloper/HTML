India

India, officially the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country by area, the most populous country, and the most populous democracy in the world. Bordered by Pakistan, China, Nepal, Bhutan, Bangladesh, and Myanmar, India is known for its rich history, diverse culture, and vibrant traditions.

History

The country was ruled by various dynasties and empires, including the Maurya, Gupta, Mughal, and British Empires. India gained independence from British rule in 1947.

Geography

India is located in South Asia and is bordered by the Himalayas to the north and the Indian Ocean to the south. Major rivers include the Ganges, Yamuna, and Brahmaputra. The country has a diverse climate, ranging from tropical in the south to temperate in the north.

Culture

India is known for its diverse languages, religions, and traditions. Major religions include Hinduism, Islam, Christianity, Sikhism, Buddhism, and Jainism. The country is famous for its festivals, cuisine, music, dance, and art.

Economy

India has a mixed economy, with agriculture, industry, and services sectors. It is one of the world's fastest-growing major economies and a leading exporter of information technology services.

Government

India is a federal parliamentary democratic republic. The President is the head of state, and the Prime Minister is the head of government. The Parliament consists of the Lok Sabha (House of the People) and the Rajya Sabha (Council of States).
