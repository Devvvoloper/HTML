HTML SEO and Tips

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is crucial for improving your website's visibility on search engines. Here are some tips to enhance your SEO:

  • Use relevant keywords throughout your content.
  • Optimize your website's loading speed.
  • Ensure your website is mobile-friendly.
  • Utilize meta tags effectively.
  • Create high-quality, engaging content.
  • Set nice URL slugs for your pages.
  • Set a nice 16*16 or 32*32 pixel favicon for your website.
  • Use compressed files and remove unwanted code and files.
  • Add alt text to images.
